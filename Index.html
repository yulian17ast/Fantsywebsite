What is fantasy?

This genre is suitable, first of all, for people with a passionate imagination and limitless imagination. And that’s why it’s useful. Fantasy stories take us beyond the usual and beyond the bounds of reason, erasing patterns and changing stereotypes. And those who are imbued with the charm of a fairy tale will want to come back more than once!
