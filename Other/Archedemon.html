Archedemon

Archdemons are the Old Gods, incarnated in the form of powerful and terrifying dragons and corrupted by the creatures of darkness. Archdemons are far more intelligent than ordinary dragons, and they are the embodiment of evil. Most of the time, the creatures of darkness exist as a hive, concerned only with increasing numbers.

They rarely appear on the surface, except during raids and minor incursions. However, the Blight begins when the creatures of darkness find and corrupt the Old God, who unites them into vast hordes and unleashes a wave of darkness and destruction on the surface.
