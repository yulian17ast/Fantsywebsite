Weapon

Weapons are devices and objects that are structurally designed to destroy a living or other target, an object for attack and, to a lesser extent, defense. Also a set of means for hunting and warfare. In a figurative sense, a weapon is any means of fighting someone or something to achieve goals.

Humanity has used weapons since ancient times. The first types of weapons were sticks and stones. From the very beginning, the main purpose of weapons was hunting and protection from predators. Later, weapons began to be used for battles between people.

Since combat plays a prominent role in most role-playing games, weapons as a tool for combat also receive significant attention. In role-playing games, you can find both melee and throwing weapons, more typical of such genres as fantasy and games based on the historical ancient world and the Middle Ages (but also found, for example, in space opera), and firearms, typical for games based on the recent historical period, modernity and post-apocalypse.
