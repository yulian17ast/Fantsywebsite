Ranged Weapones

A crossbow is a weapon that is a bow equipped with mechanisms for cocking and releasing the bowstring. It is superior to the bow in accuracy and destructive power, but inferior to it in rate of fire. For shooting from a crossbow, bolts were used - special arrows, thicker and shorter than bow arrows, and less often - bullets.

A bow is a small weapon, which is an arch with a bowstring for throwing arrows. The bowstring is pulled by hand. The arc, as a rule, is made from specially selected types of wood - yew, ash, acacia. Despite the fact that the bowstring is pulled onto the arc only before the fight, such a bow does not last very long - in a tense state, the tree quickly loses its elasticity and becomes deformed.

Pistol - a massive pistol, the favorite weapon of horsemen and pirates. Often carried in pairs, although some warriors carry three pistols or even more into battle! The pistol is a fairly heavy and strong structure so that after a shot it can be used to push the enemy away with it (if necessary). Many pistols are designed to be used as a baton.

Boomerang is a specially curved and processed throwing baton. The boomerang does not return to the owner when hit. The boomerang can be used as a club. In a fantasy setting, the boomerang returns to its owner even after being hit!

A sling is a cord or strip of material with a pocket or cup for projectiles. The shooter places the projectile in the cup, grasps both ends of the cord in his fist and unwinds it, then releases one end, releasing the projectile.
