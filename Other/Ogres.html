Ogre

Ogres are mythical cannibal giants. They appear in some classical literary works, but are most often mentioned in European medieval fairy tales and legends as negative characters as cannibals who prefer children and infants. In myths, ogres are often depicted as inhumanly large, strong, with a disproportionately large head, thick hair, unusual skin color and an insatiable appetite.

Fierce orc commanders, ogres can use combinations of their skills to reach enemy units in a matter of moments and crush them with a few powerful blows.
