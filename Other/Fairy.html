Fairies

Fairies are fragile creatures that live near picturesque elven lakes. Their main task is to care for the lake flora and fauna and, if necessary, protect it. The image of a fairy as an exquisitely attractive, usually miniature woman, was formed during the heyday of romanticism in Western literature and was developed in the Victorian era.

In a broad sense, “fairies” in Western European folklore usually mean the whole variety of related mythological creatures, often radically different from each other in appearance and habits; supposedly friendly and bringing good luck, more often - crafty and vindictive, prone to cruel jokes
