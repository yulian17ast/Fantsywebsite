Characters

Humans

In general, people in fictional worlds correspond in biology and social structure to their real basis. Humans as a race are a standard, well known to the reader, against which other fictional races are compared (in the case of a “hit”, the fictional world may not have its own people). They are universal and average in most respects: they have an average build and skills, live everywhere, eat everything, can engage in any profession, have any worldview, use any military tactics.

Elves

Elves, also called the Elder Race, are one of the most ancient and powerful intelligent races of this world. All elves are tall, slender humanoids who are naturally endowed with magical talent and have a long lifespan, reaching several thousand years.

Dwarfs

Gnomes (from the Latin gēnomos - “underground inhabitant”) are dwarfs from Western European, primarily German-Scandinavian folklore, frequent heroes of fairy tales and legends, characters from the lower mythology of the peoples of Europe. According to legends, they live underground, wear beards and are famous for their wealth and skill. Dwarves, along with elves, goblins and trolls, are popular characters in fantasy literature and role-playing games.

Orcs

Orcs are fictional humanoid creatures in the folklore of the peoples of Western Europe and in modern works of fantasy. Orcs bear a close resemblance to goblins and are among the standard types of fantasy creatures.Orcs became widely known primarily thanks to the fantasy works of the English writer J. R. R. Tolkien. In particular, in his famous novel The Lord of the Rings, the orcs - a race of ugly, cruel and aggressive monsters - contrast with the elves who personify goodness.

Undead

Undead are a category of creatures that combine all variations of the living dead (not necessarily human or even humanoid). First of all, the undead are typical for fantasy, but sometimes they are found in other game worlds. This is a fairly broad category, including a wide variety of creatures - from fleshless skeletons to indistinguishable from living vampires, from completely material mummies to disembodied ghosts, from mindless zombies to powerful lich wizards.

Demons

All demons are true machines of destruction. Their bodies are designed exclusively for military action in any environment - from outer space and icy planets, to lava seas and incredibly radioactive planets. Their bodies can withstand amazingly high and equally incredibly low temperatures: from 273.16 degrees below zero to 5 million degrees Celsius. Also, their bodies are completely immune to radiation.
