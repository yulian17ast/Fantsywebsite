Shields

Buckler is a small (30-40 cm in diameter) round shield, usually metal. Used primarily with a sword or epee. It has only one handle, which is held with a fist. More often than other shields it is used for strikes.

Shield - a shield that has two handles - for the palm and forearm. Large enough to cover one third of the body.

A large shield - in addition to two handles for holding, is often equipped with a belt that is thrown over the shoulder and relieves the load on the hand. Such a shield covers the body by half or even more.

Tower shield - a tower shield that covers the entire wearer. Often used as a support for a crossbow or musket
