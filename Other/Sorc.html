Sorcerer

A sorcerer, originally a spellcaster, a charmer is a person who practices sorcery (witchcraft, magic) to influence people or nature or to obtain knowledge or wisdom through supernatural phenomena. According to some ideas, he possesses special supernatural powers from birth or as a result of an agreement with evil

spirits. The magic that a sorcerer is endowed with can make minor changes to his essence (for example, the ability to see in the dark or read in any language), and also grants the ability to use powerful spells. Unlike well-read wizards, sorcerers can supplement their magic with some elements of hand-to-hand combat.
