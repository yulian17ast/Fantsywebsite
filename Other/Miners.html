Miner

Dwarves are excellent miners, with their strength and endurance they are excellent with a pickaxe, hammer and shovel. They also instinctively sense the rock, knowing where best to dig and when to stop and prop up the tunnel roof. Landfalls and other incidents are rare. The longer the constructed shaft, the more mechanisms there will be.

From the depths, steam locomotives continuously transport trains of trolleys along rails. Some clans even use a newfangled drilling machine invented by some mechanic. But no matter how successful they may be, they will never be able to replace the skill and experience of a gnome miner.
